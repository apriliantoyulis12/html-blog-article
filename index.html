The Secret to Consistent Coding Learning from Scratch

By Admin Updated on June 19, 2026

Learning to code often feels overwhelming at first. Many people give up because they get trapped in tutorial hell, a state where we constantly watch tutorial videos without ever trying to create our own projects.

The Importance of Hands-On Practice

The key to mastering programming is consistent practice. You don't need to spend eight hours a day. Just one to two hours, done every day without fail, is enough.

In conclusion, start with small things like understanding HTML structure. Consistency is far more important than explosive intensity at the beginning.
